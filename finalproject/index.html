Grand Traverse County: A Year In The Life

I moved to Grand Traverse County one year ago. This year has had its ups and downs. I wanted to share some personal details about my year and the sights and activities I've been lucky enough to take part in.

One of the first events I completed after moving here was running the
Bayshore Marathon. It took me 4 hours and 30 minutes.
I toured a beautiful old lighthouse
In late June I was hit by a car while riding my bike. I was rushed to the hosptial in an ambulance and had 8 stitches in my knee.

 
I moved out of my apartment in the city and into a house in Interlochen
The State Park (pictured) is very scenic.
I was nervous about my first winter but it ended up being very enjoyable. I drove around to different Christmas displays which is one of my favorite holiday activities.
At the beginning of 2024, I spent a lot of time downstate due to family issues
I missed the life I had created for myself in TC but I'm truly grateful for every second I got to spend with my loved ones.
It's unbelievable how much has happened in just one year. At the end of the day I
still feel like I live in the greatest city in the world.
